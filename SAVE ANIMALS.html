Why We Should Save Animals :

Animal conservation is the protection and preservation of animal species and their habitats. It is essential to maintain the ecological balance and biodiversity of our planet. Human activities such as deforestation, climate change, poaching, and pollution have significantly impacted the natural habitat of animals, leading to a decline in their populations. It is imperative to save animals and protect their habitats to maintain the ecological balance of our planet.
